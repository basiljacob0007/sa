About Our Company

Sastry and Associates, Inc was established in 1999 by Aruna Shastry, P.E., S.E. Sastry and Associates is a Georgia State certified S-Corporation and a DBE, registered in State of Georgia, Alabama and Florida. Over the past 25 years, Sastry and Associates has designed civil and transportation infrastructure projects for federal, state, county, municipal, and private clients.

Mr Sastry has over 40 years of experience in structural engineering and has worked several major and complex bridges in the States of Georgia, Alabama and Florida. Some of the large bridges designed by him include US17 over back river in Savannah, Georgia and I-75 over Chattahoochee River in Cobb County, Georgia.

Our engineers are trained in the latest specifications (such as AASHTO LRFD, ASCE codes, AISC, International Building Code, IRC, etc.)

The CAD personnel have functional skills in the latest plan preparations and presentations delivering quality plans for clients’ acceptance and use.

Sastry and Associates believes in putting clients’ needs foremost in the company-client process and is conscientious of project delivery in a timely manner and within budget.
